Sizzling Pork Sisig Recipe

A delicious sisig served in a sizzling plate

Ingr.

  • 1 lb pig ears
  • 1 lb. pig snout
  • 2 pieces onions minced
  • 3 pieces bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 thumbs ginger crushed
  • 1 quarts water

Sisig dressing:

  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup liver spread
  • 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Combine pig ears, snout, pork belly, ginger, bay leaves, salt, and water. Boil for 1 hour. Drain.
  2. Grill the pig parts for about 5 minutes per side. Let cool.
  3. Mix all dressing ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Chop the grilled parts and combine with onion and dressing. Toss well.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
