Mt. Malindig

Mt. Malindig is the highest peak in Marinduque and a dormant volcano known for its breathtaking views. The mountain is shrouded in legends and serves as a natural haven for hikers and researchers alike. Its slopes are home to wild orchids, misty forests, and natural hot springs nearby.
