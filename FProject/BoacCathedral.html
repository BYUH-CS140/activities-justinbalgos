Boac Cathedral

Also known as the Cathedral of the Immaculate Conception, Boac Cathedral stands as a historical landmark. Built in 1792, it played a significant role during the Filipino revolution and remains a spiritual center of the province. Its stone architecture and hilltop location make it a must-visit site.
