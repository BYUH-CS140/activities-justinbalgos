Luzon Datum of 1911

Located in Mogpog, the Luzon Datum of 1911 is the geodetic reference point for all mapmaking in the Philippines. It marks the primary triangulation station used by American and Filipino surveyors in the early 1900s. The site offers historical significance, scenic views, and a geographic curiosity as the "center of Philippine geodetic surveys."
