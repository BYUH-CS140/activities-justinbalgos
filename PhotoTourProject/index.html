WELCOME TO MARINDUQUE

Welcome to the beautiful island of Marinduque, Philippines. This page will show you previews of beautiful touirist sites in Marinduque. The above picture is Mt. Malindig which holds a special place in local folklore. One popular legend tells of a beautiful princess named Maring who enjoyed hunting on the mountain. During one of her hunts, she met a nobleman named Duque. They fell in love, but Maring's father, King Alab, opposed their union. To determine Maring's suitor, the king organized a ship race among her admirers. Duque, lacking resources, couldn't participate. Maring prayed to the gods for intervention, and a storm sank the ships of the competing suitors. The king, seeing this as a sign, allowed Maring to marry Duque.
